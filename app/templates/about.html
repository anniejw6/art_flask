What is this?

Look at art. Decide what you think of its form (the structure) and content (the idea).

Why did you build this?

Art can be puzzling. Having a framework to evaluate art, even a crude one, increases its accessiblity.

Don't people really criticize this framework?

Yes. Feel free to use a better one.

Who are you?
